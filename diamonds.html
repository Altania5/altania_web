Green Diamond

Green Diamond is the leader of the Technology and Sciences Department of the Altanian Government. This also gives Green Diamond the position of Chief Operations Officer (COO) of Altanian Industries. Green Diamond is also in the posession of the Malgico Frolalshin Gem.

Blue Diamond

Blue Diamond is the leader of the People First Department. Blue Diamond is in charge of keeping tracks of current and new laws. Review the Politics page to further understand how Altanian politics work. Blue Diamond doesn't make or deny laws, but they do keep track of votes and parties. Blue Diamond is also in the posession of the Frequency Frolalshin Gem.

King Diamond

King Diamond is the Leader of Altania. They are in charge of the entire country. King Diamond was the founder of Altania. King Diamond is also in the posession of the Altania Frolalshin Gem.

Red Diamond

Red Diamond is in charge of the Military of Altania. Red Diamond can deploy any branch of the military wherever they desire. Some branches of the military are The Alexandrite Army, The Nephrite Navy, and The Topaz Troopers. Red Diamond is also in the posession of the Abileration Frolalshin Gem.

Purple Diamond

Purple Diamond is in charge of the Economy of Altania. They oversee the currency and everyting that can change the GDP of the Country. They also determin the budget of agencies and departments of the Altanian Government. Purple Diamond is also in the posession of the Balance Frolalshin Gem.
